AI Case Study: Intelligent Chatbot
The Challenge

The initial AI chatbot often misunderstood user queries, providing generic or irrelevant answers to specific questions about product returns, leading to user frustration.

My Approach

I engineered a multi-step prompt strategy. First, I implemented a "system-level" prompt to define the AI's persona as a "helpful and precise customer service agent." Then, I used few-shot prompting, providing 3-4 examples of complex user queries and ideal answers. Finally, I instructed the AI to ask clarifying questions if the user's intent was ambiguous.

The Result

This approach reduced incorrect responses by over 70% in testing. The "before" output for "I want to return my shoes" was "Please see our return policy." The "after" output became "Of course, I can help with that. Could you please provide your order number so I can look up the return eligibility for your specific pair of shoes?"

Technologies Used: GPT-4 API, Python, Flask
